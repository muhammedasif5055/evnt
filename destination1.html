Planning your wedding orparty in Paris

The city of haute cuisine – and haute couture. Home to iconic landmarks and inspiring art collections. Where strolling arm in arm along the banks of the Seine traces one of the most beautiful cityscapes in the world. It’s easy to see why so many couples fall head over heels for Paris – the city of lights, and of love.

Always elegant, often flamboyant, destination weddings and parties in France’s capital bring a dash of theatre to special occasions. The Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe and inimitable Eiffel Tower set the scene, while the city’s galleries, theatres and opera houses inspire our own event experts. Parisian weddings could be at Hôtel Plaza Athénée or Shangri-La Hotel Paris, and the châteaux tucked into the nearby countryside are amongst the most exclusive – and exquisite – party venues in Europe.
