Imaginative. Innovative. Inspiring. The defining features of a Scarlet event. Whether it’s a society wedding or private party, our event planners set the stage with exclusive destinations, bespoke styling and show-stopping entertainment. In short, we design and deliver epic events – with creative flair and attention to every detail.

Since 2010, we have been forging connections across the world. The private palazzo in Venice. The mountain-side retreat in Switzerland. Grammy Award-winning musicians and Michelin-starred chefs. From our London headquarters, we travel the world for our discerning clients, always in search of the spectacular. Along the way, we have become choreographers of one-of-a-kind celebrations – taking the lead in every detail of each client’s very personal journey, from first step to final dance. Telling stories. Stirring emotions. Making memories.
